How does the AI work?

Minimax with Alpha-Beta Pruning — The AI explores every possible future game state as a tree. It assumes both players play optimally: it maximises its own score and minimises yours.

Alpha-Beta Pruning cuts off branches of the tree that cannot possibly affect the final decision, making the search significantly faster without changing the result.

  • Easy — AI picks a random available cell
  • Medium — Minimax limited to depth 3
  • Unbeatable — Full Minimax + Alpha-Beta (depth 9)
